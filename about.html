About TBD Academy

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Since 2011, TBD Academy has been at the forefront of quality education in Makurdi, nurturing young minds and building character for over 15 years.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops the intellectual, physical, social, and moral capacities of every student, preparing them to become responsible citizens and leaders in their communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Benue State, recognized for academic excellence, character development, and producing well-rounded individuals who positively impact society.

Our Foundation

Core Values

These values guide everything we do at TBD Academy

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in academics, character, and service.

Integrity

Honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our interactions.

Respect

Valuing diversity, treating everyone with dignity and consideration.

Innovation

Embracing new ideas and technologies to enhance learning experiences.

Our Campus

World-Class Facilities

Modern infrastructure designed to support comprehensive learning

Smart Classrooms

Air-conditioned classrooms equipped with interactive whiteboards, projectors, and modern learning tools.

Science Laboratories

Fully equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with modern apparatus and safety equipment.

Digital Library

Extensive collection of books, e-books, and digital resources with comfortable reading spaces.

Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with high-speed internet for digital literacy and coding classes.

Sports Complex

Football field, basketball court, and indoor sports facilities for physical education and recreation.

Cafeteria

Clean, hygienic cafeteria serving nutritious meals and snacks throughout the school day.
