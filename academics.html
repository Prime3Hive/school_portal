Academic Programs

Comprehensive Education for Every Stage

From early childhood to junior secondary, our curriculum is designed to nurture intellectual growth, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Our Programs

Academic Levels

Nursery (Pre-Primary)

Ages 3-4 years | 2 Sections

Our nursery program provides a warm, nurturing environment where young learners begin their educational journey. We focus on play-based learning, social skills development, and building foundational literacy and numeracy skills.

Subjects:

Literacy Numeracy Creative Arts Physical Education Social Skills

Primary School (Primary 1-6)

Ages 5-11 years | 3 Sections per Grade

Our primary program builds strong academic foundations in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking. We foster creativity, curiosity, and a love for learning while preparing students for secondary education.

Subjects:

Mathematics English Language Basic Science Basic Technology Social Studies Christian Religious Studies Physical & Health Education Creative Arts Hausa Language Computer Studies

Junior Secondary School (JSS 1-3)

Ages 11-14 years | 2 Sections per Grade

Our junior secondary program prepares students for senior secondary education with comprehensive subject offerings, exam preparation, and career guidance. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and independent learning.

Subjects:

Mathematics English Language Basic Science Basic Technology Social Studies Christian Religious Studies Physical & Health Education Creative Arts Hausa Language Computer Studies Business Studies Home Economics Agricultural Science
Academic Year

Term Structure

Our academic year consists of three terms spanning 9 months

First Term

September - November

3 months of intensive learning, assessments, and character development.

Second Term

January - March

Continuation of curriculum with mid-year assessments and progress reviews.

Third Term

April - June

Final term with comprehensive exams, promotions, and graduation ceremonies.
